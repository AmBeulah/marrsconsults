ABOUT US

MARRS CONSULTS (MC) is a complete Information Technology firm that provides a complement of sales, installation, and maintenance of computer hardware and software, training, asset management, help-desk services, bureau service,Systems Management, site preparation, Power Systems implementation, software development, IT out-sourcing, and consulting to a wide range of customers.

We are certified to support HP in the sales of HP LAPTOPS, DESKTOPS, PRINTERS, ORIGINAL HP supplies. We are also certified to support HP in the sales of HP Designjet and our company MARRS CONSULTS is the FIRST DESIGNJET ELITE CLUB (DEC) PARTNER in Nigeria.

Both hardware and software are tested before delivery; faulty equipment are professionally troubleshot, repaired and tested before re-installation. Equally, equipment are sourced and delivered to meet very tight delivery schedules as may be required by our high net-worth customers.

Become our vendor

MARRS CONSULTS intends to facilitate the establishment of a manageable size of competent, reliable and competitive vendors who are able to supply from their own sources, all of MC’s material and service requirements, wherever practical and in the process add value to MC. MC aims to shift from direct purchases from overseas manufacturers and vendors to ordering from well established, efficient and competitive, in-country Nigerian manufacturers and service providers for all product groups and work categories. Becoming an MC Vendor. Suppliers interested in pursuing business opportunities within MC are encouraged to take the following steps:

  • Register their businesses with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission or any recognized certification organizations for foreign/overseas vendors;
  • Learn more about how to do business with large corporations by attending seminars and workshops;
  • Understand MC's business.

Apply online via our SALES department mail- sales@marrsconult.com

What MC Looks For
Companies that provide high quality goods and services by MC must demonstrate the following "value-added" qualities:

  • Financial Viability
  • Superior Technology
  • Competitive Pricing
  • Customer Focus
  • Innovative business solutions
  • Excellent safety record
Pre-Qualification of Vendors

Vendors registered to do business with the MC shall undergo a pre-qualification / review process to ascertain and confirm their suitability and capacity for performing supply and/or contracting obligations for the company. Basic appraisal criteria shall include: their managerial and technical capability, supply or contracting experience, access to original equipment manufacturers (OEM’s) or reputable supply sources and/or technical partners, financial capability, access to IT communication tools, compliance to HSE requirements and other Government mandatory requirements for registration with companies in the IT Industry.

Revalidation of MC Vendors' Registration
